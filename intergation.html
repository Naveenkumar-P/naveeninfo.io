How to add ISU for an Integration?

Step 1: Click on the integration link in the confirmation page, then follow the related action to navigate to the integration and select the option to Launch/Schedule the integration.

Step 2: Before proceeding, please confirm that the desired integration has been selected. Once verified, click the "OK" button to proceed.

Step 3: On the launch page, choose the "Run with Debug Logging" option. Afterward, click on the "Inventory Load File" option.

Step 4: After clicking on the "Inventory Load File" option, select the "Create Supporting Document" option.

Step 5: On the "Create Support Document" page, attach the Excel file containing the data you wish to load. Once the file is attached, click "OK" to proceed.

Step 6: After verifying all the details on the launch page, click "OK" to initiate the integration.

How to make the schedule is owned by ISU?

Step 1: Select the integration link's related action, and select the "Reports" and then "Scheduled Future Processes".

Step 2: Before proceeding, please ensure that you have selected the desired integration. Once you have confirmed the selection, proceed to schedule the integration. In the run frequency option, choose your preferred schedule method. After selecting the desired schedule method, click the "OK" button to proceed.

Step 3: After selecting the schedule type, you will be directed to the next page where you can specify the time and duration for the integration to be launched. Fill in the details such as the specific time at which the integration should be launched and the end date until which the scheduling will occur. Provide the necessary information to complete the scheduling process.
