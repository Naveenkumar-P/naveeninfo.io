How to add ISU for an Integration?

Step 1: Select the integration link's related action by clicking it. Navigate to workday Account and select edit.

Step 2: After selecting edit, a micropage will display where you can select your preferred ISU and then click OK.

How to make an integration template deprecated?

Step 1: Click the integration link to select the related action. Go to Deployments and select Mark Instance as Do Not Use.

How to create schedule for an integration?

Step 1: Click the integration link's related action and select Integration from the menu that appears and then click on Launch/ Schedule.

Step 2: After choosing Launch/Schedule that will direct you a micro page where you need to enter Run Frequency and click "OK". Without fail, fill out every field that is required. Click "OK" after adding the details as shown in the image below.

How to know the integration status?

Step 1: Click the integration link's related action , and select the integration System and then Integration Events.

Step 2: click "OK".

Step 3: This is the page where a summary of each integration event's details will be shown, along with the event's status, such as whether it was successfully completed or not.
